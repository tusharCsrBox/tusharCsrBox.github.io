FAQs

frequently asked questions

● What is IBM SkillsBuild

IBM SkillsBuild is an online platform that offers a variety of courses to help students develop skills in emerging technologies, business, and professional development.

● How do I create an account on IBM SkillsBuild?

Visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the registration process by providing the required information.

● Is there a cost associated with using IBM SkillsBuild?

No, IBM SkillsBuild is a free platform for students to access a wide range of courses and earn certificates.

● What types of courses are available on IBM SkillsBuild?

IBM SkillsBuild offers courses in technology, business, and professional development, covering topics such as data science, artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and more.

● How do I enroll in a course?

Log in to your IBM SkillsBuild account, browse the course catalog, and click on the desired course. Click "Enroll" to start the course.

● Can I earn certificates upon completing a course?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course, you will receive a certificate that validates your achievement. Certificates can be downloaded and shared on professional platforms.
