Our Best Services

The IBM SkillsBuild platform provides a wide range of services which establishes the following value chain:

Learn Grow Get a Job of your Dreams

The following are wide range of services which SkillsBuild provides:

Psychometric Analysis

The first thing we’ll ask the student to do is complete a MyInnerGenius assessment. This online assessment tool focuses on finding the student’s strengths & building a curriculum to make the most of them.

Learning Path & Channel

You receive personalized learning recommendations which offers many of learning channels & paths which will align to your career curated by experts, Watson AI or universities like you.

In-Module Assessment

Evaluation test at the end of learning-activities to check undertsanding. Student get these IBM certified badges & certificates which are industry recognized to showcase that the student is competent in that subject.

Live Project

Live projects on the platform where you can practice the skillsets they have acquired. They can engage with students of various countries while working on the project giving them the international exposure.

Personalized Coaching

The students through the platform can identify IBM certified coaches they want to be coached by after looking at their profile and book personalized sessions with the coach.

Employment Opportunities

IBM and its network of partners also help in finding employment opportunities for the jobseekers which they can apply through the SkillsBuild Platform.
